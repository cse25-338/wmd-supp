About VYNTRA

Our Mission

VYNTRA is dedicated to preserving and celebrating the timeless elegance of classic BMW automobiles in Botswana. We source, restore, and sell the finest classic BMWs from the golden era of German engineering.

Our History

With over 20 years of experience in classic car sales and restoration, VYNTRA has built a reputation for authenticity and excellence across Botswana. Our expert team meticulously inspects every vehicle.

Our Expertise

Our team consists of certified BMW technicians and enthusiasts who understand every detail of classic BMW engineering. We source vehicles and ensure each car is mechanically sound and ready for Botswana's roads.

What Sets Us Apart

We don't just sell cars—we provide a complete ownership experience. From pre-purchase inspections to post-sale support, VYNTRA stands behind every classic BMW we sell throughout Botswana.
